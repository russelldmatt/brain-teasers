$$\frac{15}{8}$$

Joe can mow $$\frac{1}{3}$$ of the lawn per hour.  Bob can mow $$\frac{1}{5}$$ per hour.  Together they can mow $$\frac{8}{15}$$ per hour.  So it will take them $$\frac{15}{8}$$ hours to mow the lawn.
