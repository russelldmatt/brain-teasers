As a function of $$n$$, the average number of steps is

$$
\frac{n^2+n}{4}
$$

Or put another way, if $$T_n$$ in the $$n$$th triangular number (1,3,6,10,15,...), then the average number of steps is

$$
\frac{T_n}{2}
$$
